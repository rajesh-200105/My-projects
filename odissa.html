Bhubaneswar


About Bhubaneswar

Serving as the largest city and the capital of the Indian state of Odisha, Bhubaneswar is a flourishing city that flaunts a rich cultural heritage, fascinating history, and enchanting natural beauty. Popularly known as the Temple City of India, it once served as the site of seven hundred grand temples.

Lingaraj Temple


About Lingaraj Temple

Lingaraja Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva and is one of the oldest temples in Bhubaneswar, the capital of the East Indian state of Odisha. The temple is the most prominent landmark of Bhubaneswar city and one of the major tourist attractions of the state.

Puri


About Puri

The Shree Jagannath Temple in Puri, India is a 12th century Hindu temple that's 214 feet tall, making it one of the tallest monuments in India. It's located on a 10-acre stone platform in the center of town and is known for its architecture, which features vibrant wall carvings of gods and animals. Some say the temple is very clean and well-managed, and that the architecture is amazing.

Chilika Lake


About Chilika Lake

Chilika Lake is a brackish water lagoon in Odisha, India, and the largest in Asia. It is situated at the mouth of the Daya River, which flows into the Bay of Bengal, covering an area of 1,100 square kilometers. The lake is fed by 52 rivers and rivulets, and its water spread area varies between 900 to 1165 square kilometers.
