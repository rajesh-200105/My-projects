ABOUT LADAK

Tourism is one of the key economic contributors to the union territory of Ladakh in Northern India. The union territory is nestled between the Karakoram mountain range to the north and the Himalayas to the south and is situated at the height of 11,400 feet. Ladakh comprises the Leh and Kargil districts. The region boasts prominent Buddhist sites and supports an ecotourism industry. Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport at Leh offers flights from Delhi year-round via Indigo, Vistara, GoFirst, and Air India. Air India also operates weekly flights to Jammu and Srinagar.
