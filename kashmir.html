Skiing Courses in Gulmarg

Skiing in Gulmarg is a lifetime experience. Gulmarg turns into a winter fairyland with its beautiful landscape that receives snow all through the winter. Its ski slopes are ideal for both beginners and seasoned skiers. From beginner slopes to backcountry skiing, Gulmarg offers a complete range for ski lovers. Its snow quality is considered one of the best in the world and is comparable to Switzerland. The ski slopes of Gulmarg reach the thrilling backcountry with pine trees, open alpine bowls, and virgin peaks. Gulmarg skiing is done at one of the highest altitudes in the world, reaching 3979 meters in two phases. The first phase is from Gulmarg at 2600 meters to Kongdoori at 3080 meters. The second phase, which has 36 cabins and 18 towers, takes passengers to a height of 3979 meters on the Apharwat Peak. A chair lift system connects Kongdoori with Mary's shoulder at 3400 meters, taking skiers to Alpine. As the snow starts gathering in December, skiers from across the globe visit Gulmarg, and this place remains full of winter activities until the middle of March.
