Bir Billingin Himachal Pradesh

Gear up for an unforgettable experience as you enjoy paragliding in Bir Billing, a beautiful destination nestled amidst the Himalayas. Reach the activity location, meet your professional guide, and attend a briefing session for safe activity. Soar over Dhauladhar ranges, panoramic water streams & lush green fields, and take in the scenic views of nature. Admire the breathtaking vistas of the beautiful village as you fly at a height of 8200 ft. from the ground. Book Paragliding In Bir Billing and have an adventurous feel while enjoying this thrilling activity for 20 minutes.

Village Bir is located 30 km of famous hill town Palampur in Himachal Pradesh. Bir is a home for Tibetan refugee's having many monasteries a round Bir villages - Chountra, Bhattu, Tashi Jong. Town of Bir now considered as center of Eco tourism, spiritual studies, meditation and adventure Tourism (Paragliding, Camping, trekking and mountain Biking). Bir is located in Kangra District of Tehsil Baijnath in Himachal Pradesh. Village Bir located 70 km south - east from Macleodganj (Capital of Tibet in exile and home of Dalai Lama), journey takes two hours to reach.
