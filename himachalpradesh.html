Bir Billingin Himachal Pradesh

Gear up for an unforgettable experience as you enjoy paragliding in Bir Billing, a beautiful destination nestled amidst the Himalayas. Reach the activity location, meet your professional guide, and attend a briefing session for a safe activity. Soar over the Dhauladhar ranges, panoramic water streams, and lush green fields, taking in the scenic views of nature. Admire the breathtaking vistas of the beautiful village as you fly at a height of 8200 feet from the ground. Book paragliding in Bir Billing and have an adventurous feel while enjoying this thrilling activity for 20 minutes.

Village Bir is located 30 km from the famous hill town Palampur in Himachal Pradesh. Bir is home to Tibetan refugees, with many monasteries around Bir villages like Chountra, Bhattu, and Tashi Jong. The town of Bir is now considered a center for ecotourism, spiritual studies, meditation, and adventure tourism, including paragliding, camping, trekking, and mountain biking. Bir is situated in the Kangra District of Tehsil Baijnath in Himachal Pradesh. It is located 70 km southeast from McLeod Ganj, the capital of Tibet in exile and the home of the Dalai Lama. The journey takes around two hours to reach.
