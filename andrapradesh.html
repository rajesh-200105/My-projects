About araku

Araku Valley, also known as Araku, is a hill station and valley in the Visakhapatnam district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It's located in the Eastern Ghats mountains, about 112 km southeast of Visakhapatnam, at an elevation of about 3,200 ft. The valley is known for its coffee plantations, gardens, streams, waterfalls, and lush green forests.

About Yaganti Temple

Yaganti Temple or Sri Yaganti Uma Maheswara Temple is a temple of Shiva in Nandyal district in the India state of Andhra Pradesh. It was built according to Vaishnavaite traditions

Kurnool


About Kurnool

Kurnool is a city in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. It formerly served as the capital of Andhra State and was proposed as the judicial capital of Andhra Pradesh from 2024, although this is awaiting a Supreme Court decision. The city is often referred to as "The Gateway of Rayalaseema"

About Visakhapatnam

The port of Visakhapatnam is renowned for being home to the oldest shipyard in all of India, and with a plethora of manmade wonders and natural spectacles, Visakhapatnam is a must-visit tourist destination in South India. You simply cannot go to a coastal city like Visakhapatnam and not see its beaches.
